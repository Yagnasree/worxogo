FAQ

What precautions are taken before data reaches the LLM?

Data Privacy First – No private client information is sent to Azure OpenAI.
Local Processing – Data is handled on our side pre-prompt.
Anonymization – Identifiers are stripped before the API call.
Aggregated Only – We transmit metrics, never raw PII/PHI.
IP Restriction – The LLM endpoint is isolated from the public internet.
Secure Communication – All traffic is TLS-encrypted.

How is bias handled in AI outputs?

We leverage Azure AI Security’s bias-filtering services to detect and mitigate harmful bias, ensuring fair and inclusive content.

Will Gen AI replace managers?

No. Worxogo’s AI is a co-pilot that removes grunt-work so managers can invest more time in coaching and culture-building.

Is my data used to train public models?

Never. Your data stays within our secure tenancy; foundation-model inference happens in an isolated environment.
