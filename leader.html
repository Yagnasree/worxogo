With Broadcast, leaders can quickly share important updates, process changes, or motivational messages across the team or organization. Whether it’s announcing a sales competition, rolling out a policy update, or reinforcing cultural priorities, Broadcast ensures the message shows up front and center on the employee’s app.

  • No emails lost in inboxes.

Ask. Listen. Respond.

With Surveys, leaders can easily check the pulse of their teams – new processes, training effectiveness, onboarding experience, or anything else that needs attention. It’s a seamless, low‑friction way to listen, directly through the same app your team already uses daily.

  • Set up and send surveys by role, team, or business unit—no IT help needed.
  • Nudges ensure high response rates with timely reminders.
  • Spot patterns: See how sentiment aligns with performance data.
  • Zoom in on outliers: Identify where experience differs across groups.
  • Take smarter action: Focus on what actually improves engagement and performance.
