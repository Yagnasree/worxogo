Points to Prioritize

Turn business priorities into daily actions

Every action earns points based on its alignment to performance goals. These aren’t just scores; they’re visual signals that help employees quickly understand which KPIs carry more weight—guiding effort toward high-impact work.

Leaders get instant clarity and shared direction—everyone’s energy flows toward the objectives that matter most.

Badges that Build

Reinforce the right behaviours, not just results

Crossing milestones, showing consistency, or hitting goals triggers automatic badge celebrations—micro-moments of recognition that snowball into momentum and lasting habits.

According to Gallup, employees who receive regular recognition are 4× more engaged and 21 % more productive.

Leaderboard

Context beyond competition

The leaderboard makes performance transparent—overall and KPI-by-KPI. Reps self-correct, managers spot trends and coach in real time, and senior leaders get a birds-eye view of entire business units at a glance.

  • Instant visibility into upward or downward momentum
  • Data-driven coaching cues for managers
  • Celebrates peer-level wins, driving healthy competition
Quiz

Fast, focused learning for busy teams

Short, under-2-minute quizzes land right inside the flow of work. Create them fast, track participation, and reinforce knowledge through repetition—not lectures.

  • No complex setup or reporting tools
  • Nudges drive participation & overcome resistance
  • Drill into performance by individual, team, or over time
Banners & Noticeboard

Recognition & reach where work happens

From celebrating “Performer of the Week” to sharing CEO shout-outs or process updates, Banners and Noticeboards keep everyone in the loop—visually, publicly, and without email overload.
